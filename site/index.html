Divs vs Sections

This is a div. There are many like it but this one is mine. You can have disv within divs within divs within divs. Divs are the fundamental building blocks of sites. They don't need to share any theme besides anything attributed to an assigned ID or class.

This is a section. You can also have sections within sections within sections. Sections are thematic grouping of content. Examples of sections would be chapters, the various tabbed pages in a tabbed dialog box, or the numbered sections of a thesis.
